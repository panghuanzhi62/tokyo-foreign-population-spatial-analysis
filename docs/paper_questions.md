# Paper Questions

## Explanatory paper

Main question:
How do station accessibility, land value, and additional local urban-context variables jointly shape the spatial concentration of foreign residents across municipalities in the Tokyo metropolitan mainland area?

Sub-questions:
1. Does adding urban-context variables improve the baseline OLS model?
2. Does residual spatial autocorrelation decrease after model expansion?
3. Which variables show meaningful local heterogeneity in MGWR?
4. How should Kawaguchi, Edogawa, and similar municipalities be interpreted beyond a simple cost-only narrative?

## Predictive paper

Main question:
Can municipality-level urban, accessibility, land-related, and contextual variables help predict future foreign population concentration or concentration growth patterns in metropolitan Tokyo?

Sub-questions:
1. Which features matter most for prediction?
2. Can prediction outperform simple baseline models?
3. Which municipalities are most likely to intensify as future concentration areas?
4. Which predictive patterns are stable enough to motivate later scenario simulation?
